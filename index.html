FAQ

Common Questions

Is the free assessment really free?
Yes — completely free, no credit card required. You fill out the form, our coaches review it, and send you a personalised assessment and roadmap. You decide after that whether you'd like to join a paid plan.
How does online coaching work?
For online coaching, you receive a structured PDF program or get access to the Spur.fit app. Live coaching packages include sessions via Zoom or Google Meet where Coach Emman trains you directly. All communication happens via email/chat within working hours.
I'm a complete beginner. Will this work for me?
Absolutely. EP Fitness coaches clients of all experience levels — from complete beginners to competitive athletes. Your program is built specifically for where you are now, not some assumed baseline.
What's included in the nutrition assessment?
Every sign-up includes a complimentary nutrition assessment from Coach EJ Patricio, a Registered Nutritionist Dietitian. This covers dietary analysis, goal setting, and initial personalized recommendations. For full monthly nutrition coaching, see our nutrition packages.
Can I train with a friend?
Yes! We offer Train-With-A-Friend rates for in-person and online sessions where both clients receive a discounted rate. Contact Coach Emman directly to arrange.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit/debit cards via Stripe for international clients, plus local payment options for Philippine-based clients. Flexible payment options (weekly, 50% installment, or full payment) are available for training and online coaching packages.
